Our Story

Founded in 2005, Grand Hostel began as a small 12-bed accommodation with a simple mission: to create a welcoming space where travelers from around the world could connect, share stories, and feel at home. What started as a passion project between three travel enthusiasts has grown into one of the city's most beloved hostels, now hosting over 10,000 guests annually.

What Sets Us Apart

Local Expertise: Our staff are passionate locals who provide authentic recommendations beyond the tourist trails
Community Focus: Weekly events including city tours, cooking classes, and game nights

Sustainability Commitment: We prioritize eco-friendly practices including solar panels, recycling programs, and locally sourced breakfast items
Safety First: 24/7 security, individual lockers, and female-only dorms available

Our Team: Meet the heart of Grand Hostel! Our diverse team of 15 staff members comes from 8 different countries, bringing a wealth of travel experience and cultural perspectives. From Marco at reception who speaks 4 languages to Chef Anna who prepares our famous international breakfast buffet, each team member is dedicated to making your stay unforgettable.

Community Impact We're proud to be more than just a hostel. Through our "Travel with Purpose" program, we organize monthly volunteer opportunities for guests to give back to the local community. We also partner with local artisans to showcase their work in our common areas and support neighborhood businesses through our recommendations.

Our Vision for the Future As we look ahead, Grand Hostel remains committed to our core values while continuously improving our services. Upcoming initiatives include expanding our sustainable practices, creating more private room options, and developing digital resources to help guests connect before they even arrive.
