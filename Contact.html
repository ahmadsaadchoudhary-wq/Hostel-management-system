Location & Directions

How to Find Us

We are located in the heart of downtown, just a 5-minute walk from Central Station.

By Public Transport

  • From Central Station: Take exit B, walk straight for 3 blocks
  • Bus lines: 12, 24, 45 - Stop at "Traveler's Square"
  • Metro: Green line to "Downtown Central" station

By Car

  • From highway: Take exit 15 towards downtown
  • Parking: Public parking available on adjacent streets
  • Valet service available for guests
